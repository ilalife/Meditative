Who we are

It is an undertaking of the congregation of the Rogationists of the Heart of Jesus. It was established in 1897 in Italy. The founder of this congregation is St. Hannibal Mary Di Francia. The Rogationists render their service in more than twenty countries in different continents. Our mission is to create a society of balanced individuals who can be good parents, children, teachers, teenagers leaders, adults and elderly. This eventually creates a balanced co-existence of the individuals from different casts and creed in the society. Yoga is an ancient science which can be practiced by any individual. The scientific approach of yoga deals with the physical, psychological and spiritual aspects of the individual holistically and helps him to lead a balanced life. We use this yogic approach to enhance the quality of life of every individual.
